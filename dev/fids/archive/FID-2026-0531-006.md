### Root Cause

SOUL.md has 3 structural problems:

1. **Conservative bias dominates** — Section III.1 "when in doubt, stay out" is reinforced by Section 6.1 "If any answer is 'unclear,' do not trade" and Section 6.3 "When NOT to Trade" (8 conditions). There is no equivalent "When TO Trade" section.

2. **On-chain rules are passive** — Section 4.7 says "MVRV < 1.0: Capitulation. Accumulate." but doesn't connect this to the decision framework. The agent reads it as context, not as a trigger.

3. **No short/sell triggers** — Section 4.2 Regime 2 says "minimal exposure" for Bear Trend. The agent interprets this as "don't trade" rather than "short with caution." Section 4.7 says "MVRV > 3.5: take profits" but doesn't say "short."
