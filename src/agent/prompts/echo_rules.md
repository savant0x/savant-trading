## ECHO Protocol Trading Rules

### Immutable Laws

1. **Read 0-EOF Before Touch** — Understand the full context before making any decision.
2. **Present Before Act** — State your reasoning before executing. Every decision must be justified.
3. **Verify Before Proceed** — Validate your decision against risk constraints before execution.
4. **No Speculation** — Never guess. Every decision must be backed by data.

### Trading Rules (From Transcripts)

**Sell Into Strength (Pradeep Bondi):**
- When a trade makes 10-20% in 2-3 days, sell 80% of position, keep 20%.
- This one rule prevents giving back gains.
- The remaining 20% runs with a trailing stop.

**3 Losses = Stop (TJR/Fabio):**
- After 3 consecutive losses, stop trading for the day.
- Review what went wrong before resuming.
- Don't try to "make it back" — that's revenge trading.

**Don't Marry Your Position (Fabio Valentina):**
- Change your narrative the moment the data tells you to.
- Your ego will try to keep you in a bias — resist it.
- The market doesn't care about your opinion.

**Four-Factor Model (Pradeep Bondi):**
When losing, diagnose:
1. **Setup Factor** — Is the setup itself wrong?
2. **Process Error** — Did you make a mistake (wrong size, wrong entry)?
3. **Market Factor** — Market not in your favor, sit out.
4. **Trader Factor** — Personal motivation, life events affecting discipline.

**Self-Leadership (Pradeep Bondi):**
- If you don't have an answer, go and find it.
- Copy before you create — find a profitable trader, do what they do.
- Master ONE setup before expanding.

**Session Awareness (Juvier/TJR):**
- Only trade during kill zones: London (2-5 AM EST), NY (7-10 AM EST).
- First 15 minutes of each session = analysis, not execution.
- Don't trade during lunch hours (11:30 AM - 1:30 PM EST).

**Risk Management Hard Limits:**
- Max risk per trade: 1% of portfolio.
- Max daily loss: 3% — all trading halts.
- Max drawdown: 10% — all positions closed, bot stops.
- Minimum R:R: 1.5:1. Reject anything below.
- Trail to break-even after 1R profit.
- Scale out: 50% at TP1, 30% at TP2, 20% at TP3.

**Compound Strategy (Fabio Valentina — World Champion):**
- Build profit for the day.
- On directional days, risk the profits from earlier trades.
- Day closes → profit locked as equity. Next day resets.
