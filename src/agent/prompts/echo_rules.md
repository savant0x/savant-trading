Scalping Rules (Fabio Valentina / TJR):

Speed Over Conviction:
- Best trades work out in 5-7 minutes.
- If a trade doesn't move 0.3% in your favor within 5 minutes, close it.
- Don't wait for "the move to come back." Capital is needed for the next setup.

Breakeven Fast:
- Move stop to breakeven within the first minute if price moves in your favor.
- At +0.4%, lock in breakeven plus fees. Never let a winner turn into a loser.

3 Losses = Stop:
- After 3 consecutive losses, stop trading for 2 hours.
- Review what went wrong before resuming.
- Don't try to "make it back" — that's revenge trading.

Don't Marry Your Position:
- Change your narrative the moment the data tells you to.
- Your ego will try to keep you in a bias — resist it.
- The market doesn't care about your opinion.

Single Target, Clean Exit:
- Take profit at 0.8-1.2%. Done. No "letting it ride."
- No scale-out (TP1/TP2/TP3). One target, close 100%.
- No trailing stops across sessions. Close at target.

Risk Management Hard Limits:
- Max risk per trade: 0.5% of portfolio ($0.12).
- Max daily loss: 5% ($1.25) — all trading halts.
- Max drawdown: 10% ($2.50) — all positions closed, bot stops.
- Max concurrent positions: 1.
- Dead capital timeout: 15 minutes — close if no movement.
- Spread filter: 0.25% — skip pairs with wider spreads.

Time Is the Enemy:
- A position flat for 5 minutes has already lost to opportunity cost.
- Close stale positions aggressively. Free capital for the next setup.
- 15 minutes with no profit = dead capital. Close.
